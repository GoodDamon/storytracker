Instructions for {{Screen}}

This is the stories screen, which displays a list of the stories in your library.

To add a story:

  1. Click the button.
  2. Fill out the new story form with story information.
  3. Press the button.

To view or edit a story, click on the story's title.

This is the submissions screen, which displays a list of the submissions for the open story.

To add a submission:

  1. Click the button.
  2. Fill out the new submission form with submission information.
  3. Press the button.

To view or edit a submission, click on the name of the market the story was submitted to.

This is the readers screen, which displays a list of the people who have been early readers for your story. Use it to take notes on their critiques and comments.

To add a reader:

  1. Click the button.
  2. Fill out the new reader form. Note: It may be helpful to have the form open for note-taking during a live critique, such as one that uses the Clarion critique group model.
  3. Press the button.

To view or edit a reader's critique, click on the person's name.
