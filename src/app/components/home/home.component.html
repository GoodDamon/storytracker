StoryTracker

StoryTracker is a free tool to track and organize your stories.

As a writer, I needed a way to keep track of my stories and story submissions. I wanted something with more options than a spreadsheet, but without the complications so common in online trackers. My needs were simple:

  • A place to track stories...
  • ...and their submissions...
  • ...and their first-draft readers.

That's it. Nothing else.

Yet surprisingly, it was complicated to accomplish that with a spreadsheet. So StoryTracker was born!

StoryTracker aims for simplicity, while giving you plenty of features for keeping track of story and story submission metadata. Never accidentally send a story to two markets at once again! Never accidentally double submit to a single market again! StoryTracker keeps track of what is currently where, so you don't have to.

Awesome! Sign me up!

...Wait, tell me more.

StoryTracker divides the metadata for your stories into three sections.

Stories
Here you can view and edit details about your story itself, including:
  • Title
  • Genre
  • Words
  • Finished state (first draft, editing, finished, and so on)
  • Comments
You'll also see various statistics derived from other sections, such as the number of times the story has been submitted, how many first readers it has, the current market it's at, and so on.
Submissions
The Submissions section is all about marketing and selling your story. You can see at a glance where the story has been, which markets have already rejected or purchased it, what the sale price was, and a summary of earnings the story has brought you, among other details.
Readers
In Readers, you'll keep track of your story critique notes and who has already read your story. Perfect for writing groups!
